## Description
Portol is your personal command center — track finances, health, assets, documents, and daily habits for yourself and your entire household in one place.

**AI-Powered Intelligence**
- Tell Portol what you ate, how far you ran, or what you spent — it logs everything automatically
- Smart asset valuations using live market data
- Automatic categorization and profile routing

**Complete Life Management**
- Track expenses, subscriptions, and net worth
- Log health metrics, nutrition, and fitness
- Manage vehicles, property, and high-value items
- Store documents with expiration tracking
- Set goals, habits, and recurring tasks

**Multi-Profile Support**
- Separate profiles for family members, pets, and assets
- Each profile has its own trackers, documents, and data
- Filter any view by profile — zero data leakage

**Built for Privacy**
- Your data stays in your account
- No ads, no data selling
- Row-level security on every query
